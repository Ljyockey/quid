How to play

Hide

Alerts and prompts will guide you through the game. If you cancel out of one of the prompts, you must refresh the page to start the game over. In each prompt, you will be given a list of options. You must provide one of the options as your answer or else the prompt will continue to popup. During the game, your answers will cause either you or your opponent to score points. You will be prompted to select either Student, Bludger, or Snitch. You can select bludger or student as many times as you like. Once you select snitch, the game will end.
